Get Started

Do you want to learn how to play the piano? Look no further! I am Tetyana Guthrie, a highly trained and experienced pianist and educator. With over 20 years of professional experience, I specialize in teaching students of all ages and skill levels, from beginners to advanced. My traditional approach, learned from my training in Ukraine and Russia, prepares students for competitions and concerts. Lessons are flexible and can be arranged at your home, the University of Denver, or at Pianopathways music store in Littleton. Rates start at $30 for 30 minutes and $50 for 60 minutes, depending on your needs and level. And the best part? Your first session is free to see if we are a good fit! So why wait? Sign up for piano lessons today and start your musical journey.
